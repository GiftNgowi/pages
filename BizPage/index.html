    About Us

    UDOM is Tanzania's leading multidisciplinary university, with more than 13,000 staff and 38,000 students from multiple countries. UDOM was the first university in Tanzania to welcome students of any religion and race. Empower students to fulfill their academic and professional passion and to have a transformative impact on the society through continual innovation in research and creativity.

    Our Mission

    Empowering students to fulfill their academic passion and to have a transformative impact on the society through continual innovation.

    Our Plan

    To become a premier institution of international repute that prepares students for the world and an inclusive society.

    Our Vision

    To positively contribute to the educational, economic, and sustainable development of the local communities and the country at large.
